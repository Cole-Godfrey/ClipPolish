# Governance

ClipPolish is currently maintained by a small maintainer group.

## Decision Model

- Maintainers are responsible for roadmap, architecture, and release decisions.
- Significant behavior changes (especially sanitizer behavior and privacy boundaries) require maintainer review.
- Security-sensitive changes follow the policy in `SECURITY.md`.

## Change Categories

- Routine fixes/docs/tests: one maintainer approval.
- Behavior or policy changes: at least one maintainer approval and updated tests/docs.
- Release/signing workflow changes: maintainer approval and release runbook update.
